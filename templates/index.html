Abstract

Music source separation (MSS) is a task to separate audio mixtures into individual sources (a.k.a - stems) such as vocals, drums, bass, other etc.

MSS is an important topic for music information retrieval (MIR) since it can be used for several downstream MIR tasks including melody extraction, pitch estimation, music transcription, music remixing, and so on. MSS also has several direct applications such as Karaoke and music remixing.
